*Under review at Marine Mammal Science.*

This study derives contemporary growth curves for east Australian humpback whales using aerial photogrammetry data collected along the E1 migratory corridor. Results demonstrate that individuals in the Australian population attain mature body size more rapidly than those in Northern Hemisphere populations, with implications for energy budgeting models and population recovery assessments.
